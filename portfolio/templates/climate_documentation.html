Climate Risk Dashboard Documentation

The Climate Risk Dashboard is a predicted property risk assessment based on five chronic stress factors for two time frames (2050 and 2100). The data is modeled from the CMIP5 (Coupled Model Intercomparison Project 5) following RCP (Representative Concentration Pathway) 2.6, RCP 4.5, and RCP 8.5 from the IPCC (Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change).

Available Risk Factors

Drought Stress: The drought stress score describes the change in the water balance (precipitation minus potential evapotranspiration). It is based on the Standardized Precipitation-Evapotranspiration Index (SPEI), which is modelled with the penman formula using daily information about temperature, precipitation, solar radiation, humidity, and surface pressure.
